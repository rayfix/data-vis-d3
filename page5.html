Exploring More: CO2 Emissions by Country

Looking at carbon emissions by country is useful because country borders, while being human constructs, represent policy boundaries of real consequence. Use your mouse to discover the contribution of an individual country and click on it to reveal more detail. You may see if you can find individual countries like China, India, Vietnam, Indonesia and Bangladesh. They will almost certainly play a critical role in controlling the climate crisis.
